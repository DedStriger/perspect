About

Fully independent, PerSpect Asset Management is an investment advisor company founded in 2019 in Geneva, Switzerland

We provide tailor-made expertise in financial and investment advisory to institutional and private clients

We analyze a wide range of assets within the public and private markets to offer unique and uncorrelated investment vehicles. Everything we do is purposefully structured to meet our goals for superior client service and investment performance. Nurturing a culture of intellectual curiosity compels us to challenge the status quo, disrupt long-held beliefs and uncover new insights.

Member of l’Association Romande des Intermédiaires Financiers (ARIF), a Swiss self-regulatory organization (SRO) and FINMA approved regulatory body.
